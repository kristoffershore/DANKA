About

Danka is an independent reggae band from Jacksonville, FL, creating a sound that blends live-band energy, memorable hooks, and a loose, jam-like feel, anchored by a reggae backbone. Whether at a packed local bar or a stripped-down session, the band brings a performance-first approach made for audiences who want something real, entertaining, and alive. Founded by local musician Matt Henderson, Danka can be booked as a solo act, a duo, a trio, or a full band.

Available for venue bookings, festivals, private events, and recorded live sessions.
